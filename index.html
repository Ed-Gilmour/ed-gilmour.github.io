Resume

I'm an experienced Unity and C# developer, and game designer, with a strong focus on creating polished, engaging gameplay systems. I've been developing games for more than four years and have released multiple titles on Steam. Through these projects I've gained hands-on experience with scalable system design, multiplayer networking, the full release cycle of a commercial game, and much more. While most of my published projects are 2D, I also have experience in 3D game development.

I enjoy working in small, collaborative teams, but I'm also confident and effective as a solo developer. Beyond programming and game design, I have skills in Blender, Aseprite, Audacity and more, as well as a strong eye for game visuals.

I founded Simply Artizan in 2022 and right now I'm working there as a solo game developer.

Outside of game development, I'm passionate about video games and tabletop games (naturally), but I also enjoy exploring creativity in other ways and learning new skills whenever I can.
